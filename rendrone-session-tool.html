How it works
Animates through each step automatically
Twinmotion tours are hosted on Epic's servers — the browser can't directly screenshot cross-origin content. Instead we use your browser's built-in screen sharing which works on any content. You share once, then every save after is instant.
1
Click Start Sharing below — a browser dialog will open
2
At the top of the dialog click Tab — each tab shows a live thumbnail preview so it's easy to find yours even with many tabs open
3
Click on this tab (RendrOne Session Tool) — it gets a blue highlight
4
Click the blue Share button — done! All future saves are instant with no dialogs
Tip: A green "Capture active" pill appears in the toolbar. After sharing, click Save Screenshot again to take your first capture.
